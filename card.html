Rabbit

Rabbits have almost 360 degree vision and can naturally turn their ears 180 degrees.

  • Scientific Name: Oryctoclagus cuniculus
  • Average Lifespan: 9 Years
  • Tropic Level: Herbivores
  • Mass: 1 - 2.5 k.g

Rabbits need lots of space to stay healthy. Young rabbits that don't have enough space to run about are more likely to break bones, according to studies. Rabbits are social creatures and are happiest in the company of their own species.
